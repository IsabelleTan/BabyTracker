## [1.0.0] — 2026-04-13

Initial public release.

### Features

- **Event logging** — record feeds (bottle or breast), diaper changes (wet/dirty/both), and sleep blocks in 1–2 taps
- **Shared between two parents** — events sync automatically; both parents see the same timeline
- **Today's summary** — live feed, diaper, and sleep totals with 7-day average bars
- **Timeline** — reverse-chronological event log with swipe-to-delete
- **Stats** — 7-day, 30-day, and all-time charts: sleep totals, session counts, average session length, wake windows, feed counts, feed intervals, and diaper counts
- **Leaderboards** — three all-time awards (Chief Log Officer, Night Shift Ninja, Number One at Number Two) and five family records (longest sleep, best/worst night, most feeds, most poop diapers)
- **Encouragement & milestones** — contextual messages, baby POV, partner recognition, and one-time milestone cards
- **Night mode** — dimmed warm display, auto-activates between 9 pm and 7 am
- **Offline support** — logs locally and syncs when back online
- **PWA** — installable on iOS and Android home screens
- **5 am day boundary** — events before 5 am count toward the previous day, matching how parents experience the night
- **Automatic deployment** — tagged releases deploy to the VPS via GitHub Actions
